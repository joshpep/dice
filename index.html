Rules

  • This game has 2 players
  • In each turn, a player rolls the dice as many times as they wish. Each result get added to their CURRENT score.
  • The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his CURRENT score gets added to his main score. After that, it's the next player's turn
  • However, if a player rolls a 1, all their CURRENT score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn
  • First player to reach 100 points on GLOBAL score wins the game
  • You can change the winning score by typing in the FINAL SCORE box
